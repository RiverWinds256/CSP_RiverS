Matpat

the theorist

Youtube creator


MatPat is one of the original youtube theorists who began creating theory videos in 2011. These videos range in topics, from theories and predictions about games and movies, to videos about color theory. He retired last year as he wanted to have a life outside of youtube and being the personality for all his channels left him with no freetime. He passed on his channels and legacy to some of his employees, which is a controversial topic in the Matpat fandom.
